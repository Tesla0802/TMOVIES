JUJUTSU KAISEN

The cheerful teenager Yuji Itador joins the school occult club for fun, though he discovers that its members are real witches and can manage the energy between the creatures to their own advantage. He hears the legend of the cursed talisman - the finger of the demon Sukuna, on which everyone hunts. Eugene eats his finger to protect his friends and soon discovers that he is the bearer of magical powers. He decides to swallow Sukuna with all his fingers, allowing him to perform the exorcism and release himself.
